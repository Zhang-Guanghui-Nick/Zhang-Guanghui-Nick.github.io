About Me

Dr. Guanghui ZHANG received his Ph.D. degree from the Department of Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2020, and then worked as a Research Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University. Prior to that, he received his M.S. degree from Peking University in 2016.

His research broadly lies in applying state-of-the-art machine learning techniques to video streaming systems to drastically improve the viewer-watching experience (i.e., Quality-of-Experience). In the past five years, as the first or corresponding author, he has published more than ten research papers in top journals/conferences (e.g. IEEE TMC, TPDS, TWC, etc.). In addition, he has been invited as a reviewer for several top journals/conferences in the field of computer networking (e.g. IEEE TMC, IEEE/ACM ToN, etc.).
